Design Practices Well Implemented

  1. Content Presentation: The content provided is concise and paints a vivid picture of what guests can expect. It makes use of techniques like bullet points to present information clearly and in an easily digestible format.

Design Practices Needing Improvement

  1. Navigation: From the description, it's not clear how the navigation is structured. For best practices, main navigation links should be clearly and consistently labeled, and there should be aids like a site map or breadcrumbs.
  2. The use of images, especially with appropriate alt text, can enhance user experience
  3. Contact Information: While there's an address provided, it would be a good practice to also include a phone number, email address, and possibly a contact form for reservations or inquiries.

Other Improvements

Testimonials or Reviews: Consider adding testimonials or reviews from past guests to provide potential customers with insight into others' experiences. Interactive Elements: Given the luxurious description of the resort, interactive elements like a photo gallery or a virtual tour of the yurts and resort amenities could elevate the user experience. Responsive Design: Ensure that the website is optimized for various devices, especially mobile devices, for a seamless browsing experience.
